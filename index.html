About

SchnitzelSMP runs on PaperMC version 26.1.2 but allows for players on newer versions of the game to join thanks to Via Version. We are additionally using a Lifesteal Plugins paired with many custom Enchants to provide an authentic Factions experience. Currently most of our Plugins are configured public Plugins but that will change as soon as we are able to expand our budget & my proficiency in Java improves.

Join my Discord or reach out to me with support@schnitzelsmp.eu for more information.

Note: If you have trouble connecting, ensure your client supports IPv6 or use our proxy address.

Backstory

My journey in the sport commonly referred to as Calisthenics or Street Workout originally started all the way back in April 2025. Up until that point I had already been doing simple things like pushups but never took it too seriously. However things began to change when I went on a holiday trip to Portugal in late April where I discovered a video of someone doing a handstand on Social Media. This led me to achieve a handstand within the next 2 weeks by practicing daily. After that I started striving for more and have since unlocked many more skills.
