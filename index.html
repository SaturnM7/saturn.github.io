Backstory

My journey in the sport commonly referred to as Calisthenics or Street Workout originally started all the way back in April 2025. Up until that point I had already been doing simple things like pushups but never took it too seriously. However things began to change when I went on a holiday trip to Portugal in late April where I discovered a video of someone doing a handstand on Social Media. This led me to achieve a handstand within the next 2 weeks by practicing daily. After that I started striving for more and have since unlocked many more skills.
